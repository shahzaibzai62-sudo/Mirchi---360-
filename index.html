Our Story

360° of Authentic Taste

Welcome to Mirchi 360 — where every dish is crafted with love, tradition, and the finest spices. Located in the heart of Tando Adam, Sanghar, we bring you a full circle of culinary excellence.

From sizzling Karahis to mouthwatering BBQ platters, crispy fast food to comforting Desi Handi — our menu spans the full 360 degrees of Pakistani cuisine and beyond.
